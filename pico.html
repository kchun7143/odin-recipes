Pico de Gallo

Decription


This Pico de Gallo recipe requires a lot of chopping but it is worth the work.
It can be enjoyed with tacos, tortilla chips, or as a side.
I even add it to my taco salad and nachos. The possibilities are endless.
You can adjust the amount of ingredients to your liking. There is no right or wrong way.

Ingredients

  • Roma tomatoes- 6- Diced
  • Garlic- 3 cloves- minced
  • Green Onion- 1 stalk- chopped
  • Jalapeno or Serrano Pepper-1- diced
  • Half an onion- diced
  • Lime- 1
  • Cilantro- handful- chopped
  • Salt
  • Vinegar - splash
  • Olive Oil- splash

Directions

  1. Prepare all the vegetables with cut method stated on the ingredient list
  2. Place all vegetables in a bowl and season with salt, vinegar, and olive oil.
  3. Start squeezing juice of half a lime into bowl. Stir and taste test. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
