Macaroni Beef Soup

Description

This macaroni beef soup recipe is very comforting and easy to make.
This recipe will take about an hour to make and it only requires 11 ingredients.
When making Macaroni Beef Soup, the noodles can be made in a separate pot
and it can be later added to prevent the noodles from soaking up all the liquid.
I prefer my noodles to be soaked because it reminds me of Macaroni Beef T.V. dinners
that you get from the freezer aisle at the grocery store.
I hope you enjoy this dish as much as I do.

Ingredients

  • Garlic- 3 minced
  • Diced Onion- half
  • Oil - approximately a tablespoon
  • Ground Beef - appoximately a pound
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Jar of your favorite pasta sauce - 24 oz
  • Chicken Broth- 32 oz
  • Cream Cheese- 4 oz
  • 1.5 cups of Elbow Noodles

Directions

  1. Heat pot on stove with oil.
  2. Saute garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Put ground beef into put and cook until brownP.
  4. Drain oil from browned meat. Season with approximately a tsp of salt, pepper, and oregano
  5. Put spaghetti sauce into pot.
  6. Put a splash of chicken broth into the jar and swish the rest of the sauce.
  7. Put the rest of the broth into the pot. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Mix cream cheese with broth until stir until melted.
  9. Put in elbow noodles and stir. Cook covered for 10 minutes. Stir in 5 minutes.
  10. Season to taste. Noodles will soak up more liquid when it's cooling. Enjoy!
