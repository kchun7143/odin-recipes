Taco Pie

Description


Enjoy this quick and easy recipe that will please the kids.
Feel free to add extra condiments to eat with this taco pie.
For example, shredded letucce, chopped tomatoes, sour cream, etc.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Cooking spray
  • Garlic- 2 minced
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 jar of your favorite salsa
  • A package of soft tortillas
  • 2 cups of Mexican Cheese
  • Optional: Sour cream, green onions, lettuce, and/or tomatoes

Directions

  1. Spray pan with cooking spray and cook garlic until fragrant
  2. Cook ground beef until no longer pink. Drain fat when done
  3. Season ground beef mixture with taco seasoning, chilli powder, cayenne pepper, and water.
  4. Stir and cook until water has been reduced. Turn off flame.
  5. Depending on the size of your tortillas, cut them in half to assemble your taco pie.
  6. Using a 13 x 9 pan. Put a spoonful mixture on the bottom and spread.
  7. Spread 1/3 of salsa with bottom layer of meat mixture.
  8. Assemble tortillas to cover the mixture. Add more meat, 1/3 salsa, cheese, and tortilla. Repeat for 1 more layer
  9. For the last layer, make sure you top with more cheese.
  10. Bake for 20 min at 375 degrees. Taco pie will be done when cheese is melted.
  11. Garnish with green onions, sour cream, lettuce, and tomatoes. Enjoy!
